Municipality of Medellin

In the northern parts of Cebu, you will find the gorgeous town of Medellin. It is known for its many water activities that attract both tourists and locals. Not only that but there are also great historical sites and even food spots in town. Medellin is a 2nd class municipality in the province of Cebu, Philippines. It is one of the nine municipalities comprising the 4th Congressional District Cebu Province. It is 119 kilometres (74 mi) north from Cebu City. Medellin is bordered on the north by the town of Daanbantayan, to the west by the Tañon Strait, on the east by the Camotes Sea, on the southwest by the town of San Remigio and the southeast by the city of Bogo.
