MATAHOM BEACH RESORT

Published by: Kimbery Conde

LOOK: "Matahom Paradise Villas" in Brgy. Kawit in Medellin, Cebu the newest destination for the beach lovers who want an exclusive stay with family and friends. Guests who have been to the place said they felt like they were in Bali without needing to travel far and break banks.

Matahom is a Cebuano term for ‘beautiful’. True to its name, this place doesn’t fall short of the finest and grand things you can experience for a beach staycation. Everyone can pamper all day by dipping into their cold swimming pool.While soaking in the pool, you will be treated with a wide expanse of a panoramic sea view and the horizon.

And yes, Matahom Paradise Villas is also adorned with white sand beachfront where you and your group can hold exciting beach activities. Medellin is a town in the northern parts of Cebu that is also known for white sand beaches and clear waters and in this villa, you will get the taste of how it feels to be in Medellin. The palm trees that surround the place will elevate the tropical experience.

Once you book the place, you can be certain that the entire space is all yours, giving you a private spot for an intimate get-together with your friends or your family. Their front villa will let you wake up to the scenic view of their infinity pool and the beach. They also have standard rooms complete with free breakfast.
